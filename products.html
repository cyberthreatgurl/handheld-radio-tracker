    Reviews Help
    Welcome to eHam.net Reviews! Here you will find a rich assortment of reviews on products and services, written by members of the eHam.net community. If you want to read real, unbiased opinions of a product or service that you're thinking of buying, this is the place to do it! And if you have something to say, then by all means write a review!

    Finding a Review The Reviews home page gives you several ways to locate the product or service you want to explore. The Category table is the main entry to Reviews. You can browse the Category table for a heading that describes the product or service you're trying to find. Then, click the category name to display the Products page. You can scan the product names until you find the one you want.

    The most recent forty reviews are listed on the right side of the Reviews home page. This gives you a quick look at the newest Reviews on eHam.net. Click on the link and you can read one of the recent reviews.

    Finally, if you haven't found the product or service by browsing the Category and Product pages, you can use the Search Reviews box to search the text of the reviews.

    Using the Products Page On the Products page, you'll see the products and services in alphabetical order, with the number of reviews and average rating. In eHam.net Reviews, the highest rating is 5 and the lowest rating is 0.

    The most recent ten reviews are displayed on the Products page as well as the Reviews home page.

    Reading the Details The upper table shows a photo of the product with some information that you didn't see in the other pages. The MSRP is the Manufacturer's Suggested Retail Price (or list price). If the person who submitted the product knew the manufacturer's web site, this may be available as well.

    Following the summary table, you'll see the detailed reviews written by members of eHam.net. There are at most 10 reviews to a page, you will see navigation links above and below the reviews if there are more than 10 reviews. Use these links to move to the other pages.

    If you know someone who would be interested in reading a review, you can click the link in the table to email the review to them.

    Adding Product Information You can add a photo, the MSRP, a description, or the manufacturer's web site address, if they are missing from the summary table. Click the link in the summary table and then follow the instructions on the following pages.

    Adding a Product or Service On the product page, click the link to add a new product or service. Please supply as much information about the product or service as you can, and follow the instructions on the form.

    Writing a Review Click the link on the details page to take you to the form to write a new review. Please follow the instructions on the form as you write your review.

    If you have any questions, problems, or suggestions about Reviews please email your Reviews Manager

    ***PRODUCT REVIEWS TERMS & CONDITIONS
    -----------------------------------------------------------

    >>GENERAL INFO:<<

    1. eHam's Product Reviews are a database of personal opinions for ham radio related products and services

    2. There's a delay before newly submitted reviews are Approved and posted in eHam's reviews. The delay is for new review screening and approval. The delay can vary from minutes to a day or more.

    3. Before adding a new product use the "Search" tool on reviews Home page to determine if that product already exists in a category.

    4. If your new review has not been Approved after several days you can assume that it was found to not be within the product review T's&C's shown below during screening.
    ==================================

    ***FOR REVIEW AUTHORS***

    Products to be reviewed should be tangible ham radio related items such as radios, antennas, towers, test equipment, feedlines, repair service, etc.

    eHam's Product Reviews are intended to provide an opportunity to share an opinion, positive or negative, of a commercially available ham radio-related product or service.

    Don't review home brewed equipment or home-brew copies of commercially made products.

    A valid product review is one where you have personal experience or knowledge of the product that you would like to share with others.

    Product reviews are not a Forum for two-way exchanges, inquiries, for-sale, general discussions, complaints, help requests, product repairs or modifications.

    NOTE: Product Reviews are not for reviews with a primary focus on customer service or support.

    The product reviews is not the correct location for reviews of customer service, product or customer support, or reviews of companies/dealers/manufacturers. The correct location for customer service reviews is the eHam Community Forum "Company Reviews".

    Mixing review opinions together of both a product and companies/customer service results in product review Rating numbers/Stars that do not reflect valid ratings for the actual product.

    A review should provide useful relevant opinions of a product. Reviews that only state "Its great"or "Terrible product" are not helpful without information why its great or terrible.

    If using an anonymous eHam user ID for submitting reviews be aware that there has been a court ruling regarding identity disclosure of writers of false reviews.

    No obscenities, discriminatory, abusive, or other content not suitable in a public forum or for younger readers.

    Reviews are limited to one review per product for each reviewer. Multiple reviews of the same product by one person may unfairly bias/skew the product average rating numbers.

    You can add to or update/edit your existing review's content including the Rating and Time Owned with the Edit button on the right side of the review text body. Edits will go thru the review Approval process.

    Review editing is provided to change your review and correct errors, etc. It is not intended for frequent blogging type incremental review additions or updates.

    Do not submit a product review critiquing another review or reviewer. Reviewers should be able to share their product opinion without being publicly criticized.

    In the interest of maintaining objective product reviews for all review readers, requests to remove Approved product reviews may be declined by eHam.

    For protection of eHam review readers reviews should not contain links/URLs off-site with the exception of the More info URL.

    Reviews of equipment capable of CB/Freeband operation or not FCC Certified/Type Accepted may be removed.
    ==================================

    ***FOR MANUFACTURERS & DEALERS***

    Do not add your own products to a product category.

    Customers and product users will add your product to a product category listing and write reviews when they are ready to share their product opinion.

    If a manufacturer would like to announce or raise awareness for their product eHam suggests writing a brief News release and submit it to eHam's News. Click the News header on Home page then look for Add News button.

    It's a conflict of interest for manufacturers or their employees to review their own products. Those reviews will be removed.

    eHam's product reviews are not to be used for advertising, marketing, product announcements, or as a product or customer service support facility.

    Do not offer rewards for positive eHam product reviews. Offering rewards for good reviews can create detectable bias in reviews that savvy review readers can notice. This can detract from trust by current and potential customers.

    eHam considers soliciting and/or rewarding favorable reviews by manufacturers/dealers as unethical and unwelcome. Those reviews are subject to removal.

    eHam's Product Reviews are independent of eHam's advertisers.

    Reviews that have been approved should be considered as being in accordance with eHam's Product Review Ts&Cs as described here.

    If a review is posted that you believe is unfairly critical of your product and you wish to respond with useful information contact eHam's Product Review's Manager to request the addition of an "eHam.note" to the review.
